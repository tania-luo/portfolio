The project is an identity system for SAIC Freshman Show in the spring semester. It consists of a logo, the main poster, and six due date posters.

Process

I recalled my first year when ideating this poster series. The mandatory interdisciplinary practice in the first year allowed me to explore distinct directions without limitation, so I want the visual of the poster based on the diverse core practice. Each poster uses a unique set of patterns related to a specific showcase, and the intuitive composition allows them to interact.

As the patterns overlay, they create new motifs. It celebrates this welcoming and diverse community.
