Drifting

FORMAT:
ROLE:
TOOLS:

artist book
designer
Illustrator, Photoshop

Drifting document and demonstrate my personal experience of feeling lost as an international student. I use “drifting” as an analogy of studying in a foreign country. As an international student from East Asia, my parents took care of everything from elementary to high school, and they pushed me away to unfamiliar place, alone. Though the external self could migrate easily across the ocean, the internal self is still lost in the dark.
